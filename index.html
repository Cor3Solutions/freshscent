How It Works

How to Use the
Fresh-Scent Drop Box

Three simple steps and you're done. We take care of everything else.

1

Register Online or via Our App

Sign up on our website or download our mobile app. We'll mail you your personal, reusable canvas garment bag and a convenient receipt book.

2

Fill Out Your Receipt & Pack Your Bag

Fill out a slip from your receipt book listing the items you want cleaned. Place it inside your canvas bag along with your garments.

3

Drop & Go!

Simply drop your bag into the nearest Fresh-Scent Drop Box — at your grocery store, apartment lobby, or office building. That's it!

There is nothing else to do...
Just go home and rest!

We will clean your clothes beautifully and professionally, delivering them right to your home or apartment within 48 hours — freshly pressed and pristine.

Need to add special instructions? You can text or email us special care instructions, preferences, or any notes you'd like to share about your garments at any time.
